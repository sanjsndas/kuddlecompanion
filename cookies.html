📌 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device, remember your preferences, and improve functionality. Cookies are widely used to make websites work efficiently and provide analytical insights. Think of them as tiny helpers that remember your favorite pet treats or keep you logged into your adoption profile.

🍪 How We Use Cookies

Kuddle Companion Nest uses cookies for several essential and optional purposes:

  • ✅ Essential To enable core functionality like secure login, appointment booking, shopping cart for pet supplies, and form submissions.
  • 📊 Performance & Analytics To understand how visitors interact with our website (most visited pages, adoption trends, popular breeds) so we can improve content and user experience.
  • 🎨 Functionality To remember your preferences (language, region, font size, or favorite pet profiles).
  • 🎯 Targeting/Advertising To show you relevant pet-care ads, promotions, or rescue event updates (only with your consent where required).
