🐾 1. Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us deliver a safe, personalized, and tail-wagging experience. Categories include:

  • 🐕 Personal Info Name, email address, phone number, postal address, and pet preferences (e.g., dog/cat parent, breed info).
  • 📞 Communication Data Messages you send via our contact forms, adoption inquiries, support requests, or newsletter signups.
  • 💻 Technical Data IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and browsing actions on our website (e.g., pages visited, clicks).
  • 🏷️ Transactional Info If you purchase pet supplies or make donations, we collect billing/shipping details (payment data is processed by secure third-party gateways).
  • 📸 Pet-Related Information Photos, health notes, or behavioral details you voluntarily share to enhance our care recommendations.

❤️ 2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us build a better nest for every companion. Specifically, we may use it to:

  • ✅ Respond to your questions, schedule vet appointments, or process adoptions.
  • ✅ Send service notifications, appointment reminders, and safety alerts related to your pet.
  • ✅ Improve our website, develop new pet-care resources, and personalize content (e.g., breed-specific tips).
  • ✅ Process donations, orders for pet supplies, and event registrations.
  • ✅ With your consent, share newsletters, exclusive offers, and community stories (you may opt out anytime).
  • ✅ Prevent fraud, ensure website security, and comply with legal obligations.

🤝 3. Sharing & Disclosure of Information

We never sell your personal information. However, limited sharing occurs only in these circumstances:

  • 🐾 Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate our platform (e.g., hosting, email delivery, payment processing, veterinary telehealth) – they are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • 🐶 Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Kuddle Companion Nest, our users, or the public.
  • 🏡 Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred with notice.
  • 📢 With Your Explicit Consent: For any other purpose when you give us permission.

We may also share aggregated, anonymized data for research or marketing — this never identifies you personally.

🍪 4. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies (small text files) and similar trackers to enhance your experience on our website. These help us remember preferences, understand site usage, and show relevant pet-care content. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings. For more details, please see our separate Cookie Policy. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they ensure site functionality (e.g., forms and security).

📜 5. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location (including GDPR for EU/EEA, CCPA for California residents), you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • 🔍 Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • ✏️ Correction: Update or rectify inaccurate information.
  • 🗑️ Deletion: Request erasure of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • ⚙️ Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • 📤 Data Portability: Receive a structured, machine-readable copy of your data.
  • 🚫 Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from promotional emails via the “unsubscribe” link or by contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at privacy@kuddlenest.com or call our Pet Data Helpline at +1 (555) 123-4567. We will respond within 30 days. No discrimination will occur for exercising your privacy rights.

🛡️ 6. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard safeguards including encryption (SSL/TLS), access controls, regular security audits, and employee training. While no system is 100% secure, we strive to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Typically, contact information is kept for 3 years after last interaction, unless you request earlier deletion.

In the unfortunate event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by law.

🧸 7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for pet owners and animal lovers of all ages, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

🔗 8. Third-Party Links & Features

Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., veterinary partners, pet supply brands, animal welfare organizations). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before sharing any personal information.

🌎 9. International Transfers

Kuddle Companion Nest is based in the United States. If you access our services from outside the US, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US. By using our website, you consent to this transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place for data originating from the European Economic Area.

📅 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect legal, operational, or technical changes. The “Last Updated” date at the bottom indicates when the latest revision was made. We will notify you of material changes via email or a prominent notice on our website before they become effective. Your continued use of our services after the update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
